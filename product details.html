ABOUT THE DESIGNER
Created by Los Angeles-based designer Heidi Cornell McKenna, Young Fabulous & Broke clothing has gained an international reputation for embodying the spirit of California style. The brand has a knack for crafting effortless wardrobe staples that exude an easy boho style. Perhaps best known for its distinctive collection of tie-dyed maxi dresses, Young Fabulous & Broke is far-reaching in scope, offering everything from slouchy joggers to slinky black dresses.
